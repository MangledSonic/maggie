Who Am I

I am a American YouTuber who enjoys playing retro video games like Sonic, Duck Hunter, Castlevainia, and other cool games. I have played Mario on my original NES, but I hate him and every one of his friends. I also wouldn't say no to a nice game of FNaF!

Art

I really like drawing! I can draw Sonic and all his friends, all the FNaF characters, and the original Showbiz Pizza characters!

Game Design

I work on designing games for indie developers every once in a while. Always an interesting thing to do!
